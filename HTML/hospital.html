Hospital automation solutions help in revolutionizing the collection of patient data and also help to move this data in such a way that it empowers clinicians to deliver superior, evidence-based patient care. --> The term “hospital automation” is very broad, and actually means automating and integrating patient data generated from patient monitors, lab equipment, medical imaging equipment etc apart from automating the standard flow of patients in the Hospital. The strength of hospital automation lies in how integrating some aspects of healthcare, benefits clinicians, care teams, Biomed/IT, and telehealth and aftercare specialists streamline workflows, minimize manual documentation, enhance the care experience for patients, and extend care beyond the hospital and into the home.
